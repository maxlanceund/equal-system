## Steps

1. **Confirm the left**: You have completed these.
2. **Cross out the right**: All entries on the right are crossed out — they do not belong to you.
3. **Re-confirm**: When the right reappears, look only at the left, and say: **"Already here."**

---

## Principles

- The left does not need to be added to.
- The right is already crossed out — no need to walk or zero it.
- Reappear, reconfirm.
- Until it no longer needs to be confirmed.

## Origin

This method is a practical tool derived from the *Limit Theory of Meaning*, used to process "unresolved" path signals.
