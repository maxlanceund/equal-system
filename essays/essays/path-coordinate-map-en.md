# Path Coordinate Map (Position Confirmation Method)

Classify the paths in your life into two dimensions: **Executed / Unexecuted**.

You don't need to walk through any path. You only need to mark their positions.

---

## Steps

1. **List both paths**: Write down "executed" and "unexecuted" paths separately.
2. **Confirm executed paths**: Look at the left column — you don't need to do anything, just confirm they are already there.
3. **Place unexecuted paths**: Look at the right column — no need to zero them out, no need to walk them. Just confirm they are there.
4. **Confirm your position**: You have already completed everything on the left. The right column does not need to enter your system to complete you.

## Core

**You don't need to walk through it. You only need to confirm it.**

Executed paths are already in you.  
Unexecuted paths do not need to be zeroed out — they only need to be placed in their own position: outside your system, outside your responsibility.

Loss is not because "I should have been on the right."  
It is because "I am still looking at the right."

The Path Coordinate Map lets you shift your gaze from the right to the left — and confirm: my position is already on the left.
